VALORANT CHARACTERS

More than guns and bullets, you’ll choose an Agent armed with adaptive, swift, and lethal abilities that create opportunities to let your gunplay shine. No two Agents play alike, just as no two highlight reels will look the same.

Omen

Controller

Phantom • Shrouded • Lurker

A mysterious, cynical figure constantly in pain, searching for his past. He specializes in blinding enemies and obscuring vision with rechargeable, long-lasting shadow smokes.

Signature Ability: From the Shadows

Chamber

Sentinel

Rich • Gentleman • Precision

A stylish "gentleman assassin" focused on defensive gunplay, precision, and economic superiority. He utilizes custom, high-powered radianite weapons to hold angles and a teleportation anchor system for site security.

Signature Ability: Tour de Force

Fade

Initiator

Mysterious • Decay • Emo

Turkish Initiator Fade uses nightmare-based radiant powers to hunt and incapacitate enemies. She excels at gathering intel, seizing positions, and inflicting decay and deafness on opponents.

Signature Ability: Nightfall

Viper

Controller

Toxic • Poisonous • Deny Vision

Viper specializes in area denial and vision control using a fuel-based toxic gas system. She is a chemist who uses green, venomous smoke screens to damage and decay enemies.

Signature Ability: Viper’s Pit

Killjoy

Sentinel

Genius • Defensive Anchor • Site Holder

Killjoy is a "wunderkind" sentinel in Valorant who specializes in locking down sites and controlling areas using her custom-built, high-tech gadgets. She is often dubbed the "genius of Germany".

Signature Ability: Lockdown

Sova

Initiator

Hawkeye • Scouting • Information Gathering

A Russian agent designed for recon and scouting, using a high-tech bow to track enemies and provide tactical intelligence. His custom bow and incredible scouting abilities ensure that even if you run, you cannot hide.

Signature Ability: Hunter's Fury
